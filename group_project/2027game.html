Super Bowl LXI

Super Bowl LXI will take place in the city of Los Angeles, California. While every city has their own traditional game day foods. Los Angeles is known for its Mexican food, so we have chosen to feature some of the best Mexican dishes that are perfect for game day and you are bound to find on the streets and in the homes of Los Angeles.
Food and Beverages of Los Angeles
Food/Beverage Description Image
Michelada A refreshing beer cocktail made with beer, lime juice, assorted sauces, spices, and peppers. It is served in a chilled, salt-rimmed glass and is perfect for hot game days.
Margarita A classic Mexican cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and triple sec. It is served in a salt-rimmed glass and is a perfect complement to the spicy flavors of Los Angeles street food.
Moscow Mule A Moscow Mule is a cocktail made with ginger beer, vodka, and lime juice. It is served in a copper mug and is a popular drink in Los Angeles.
Jalapeno Poppers Jalapeno poppers are a popular appetizer in Los Angeles, made by stuffing jalapeno peppers with cheese and sometimes wrapped in bacon before being fried or baked.
Danger Dog A popular street food in Los Angeles, the Danger Dog is a bacon-wrapped hot dog that is typically grilled and served with a variety of toppings such as onions, peppers, and sauces.
Street Tacos Los Angeles is famous for its street tacos, which are small, flavorful tacos typically filled with a variety of meats such as carne asada, al pastor, or carnitas, and topped with onions, cilantro, and salsa.
Themed Sugar Cookies When attending a tailgate or a football party you are sure to find a football themed dessert. A common find is sugar cookies decorated to show all of the guest who the house team is. Here is an example of party where the favorite team is the New England Patriots
